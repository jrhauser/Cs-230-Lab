DRY TOPS
_______

When paired with one of our spray skirts, a dry top is built to keep you dry when kayaking. Latex wrist/neck gaskets with neoprene over cuffs create a tight seal to prevent water intrusion. Dry tops are a great, versatile addition to any kayaker's gear quiver as they excel in a variety of conditions.
